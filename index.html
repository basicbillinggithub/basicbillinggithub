    Medical Fields We Currently Service

    Below is the list of medical fields we provide our professional billing services to.

    • Oral hygienist

      Clean teeth examine patients for signs of oral diseases such as gingivitis and provide other preventive dental care. They also educate patients on ways to improve and maintain good oral health.

    • Dental therapist

      Performs all the basic much needed dental procedures, preparing and filling cavities, extractions, cleaning and polishing teeth, and x-rays.

    • General dentist

      Diagnose, treats and manages overall oral health care needs, including gum care, root canals fillings, crowns, veneers, bridges, dentures implants and implant supported procedures as well as OH preventive education.

    • Oral and maxillofacial surgeon

      Specialist who treats many diseases, injuries and defects in the head, neck, face, jaws hard and soft tissue of the mouth and maxillofacial (jaw and face) region.

    Orthodontist

    Aims to prevent, diagnose and treat facial and dental irregularities, such as malocclusions (bad bites).

    Periodontist

    Specializes in the prevention, diagnosis and treatment of periodontal diseases and in the placement of dental implants they also expertise in treatment of oral or gum inflammation.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    • How am I sure that basic billing solutions will deliver what they promise?

      We offer a three month probation period and if the practice is not satisfied with our services they have the rights to discontinue services with basic billing solutions.

    • What is the turnaround time for submitting claims and getting payments?

      Turnaround time for medical scheme payments is 7 to 30 days depending on the medical aid check runs.

    • Do I need to pay electronic claim submissions as well as ERA services?

      Depending on the package you choose, basic billing solutions have an option for practice to pay their own electronic submission and ERA services. We also offer an option to pay the services on behalf of the practice at a different cost.

    • When do you start on following up on all outstanding accounts?

      As soon as remittances are available for reconciliation, we follow up same time same day.

    • What is the percentage rate for basic billing solutions?

      We offer very low percentage rates, rates also vary from practice discipline type.

    • How much is percentage rate of bad debts written off accounts?

      Approximately 3-5% overall.

    • How much is the percentage rate of paid accounts?

      Approximately 97% overall.
